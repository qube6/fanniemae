About our careers

Why Fannie Mae

At Fannie Mae, our vision is to be America's most valued housing partner. Our culture and business priorities are aligned with this vision and we are working every day to achieve it.

What We Offer

We offer a comprehensive range of benefits including retirement and financial planning, healthcare, and a healthy work/life balance.

Locations

Headquartered in Washington, D.C. and with offices across the country, Fannie Mae offers flexible living options.

Who We Seek

We are looking to improve our workforce with a diverse and talented group of people who welcome the opportunity to be on the front line of change.

Hiring Process

Knowing about our recruitment process will help facilitate your job search. Take a few moments to review our hiring information so you know what to expect as you pursue a career with us.

University Recruiting

Join our team and put your unique talents to use as we work to improve our nation's housing finance system. Our Internship or Associate Programs give employees the ability to gain hands-on experience in various disciplines.
